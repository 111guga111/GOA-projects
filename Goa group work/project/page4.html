What Is GOA?

GOA stands for Goal-Oriented Academy. It is an online educational platform based in Georgia.
We offer modern, skill-based learning for students who want to grow personally and professionally. It focuses on discipline, goal setting, and real-world skills such as programming, design, and communication.

How To Join?

Want to join GOA Academy?
The process is simple! Just contact us on Facebook and we'll help you get started. If a friend recommended GOA to you, we highly recommend that you personally message the owner for a more direct and smooth experience.

What do you need?

To join GOA Academy, all you need is a camera, a computer, and discipline. Thats it. No expensive gear, no fancy setup — just the basics and the will to grow. With those three things, you can join GOA and be amazing at it. You are never too old to learn — and at GOA, we believe anyone can become the best version of themselves.
